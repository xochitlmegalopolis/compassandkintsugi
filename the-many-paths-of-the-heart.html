A chorus of frogs and fireflies in the dark — the heart has many paths through the thick forest of trees branching ways between, against branching ways from earth upward toward the vacant sky of storms where lightnings flash. Several sounds at once can ride the tossing breeze Several lights can pulse at odds, or signal synchrony Meanwhile the world in shadows sleeps and time is made material where choice decides to be come magnetized to slopes unfolding leaves But how does one below the rising hills keep knowledge of north, west, south and east fresh in the memory, as the mind's trail weaves?
